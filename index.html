This is a Computer, it is comprised of 7 parts, Those parts are one of the following; Case, Motherboard, CPU, GPU, RAM, PSU
This is the Motherboard, it's the body of a computer, it holds the computer parts required to run a desktop.
This is the CPU, it's basically the Brains of the Computer. it handles everything from your programming, to gaming, to inputs/outputs, it handles everything, without it your pc is useless This example is a R7 7800X3D
This is the GPU, it's like the eyes of the PC, without it, you wont be able to see an image on a monitor. it handles parallel computations.
This is the RAM, it's main use is to keep the most frequently used data at a quickly accessible area, ensuring no delays. if you put data on ssd instead of ram when you use it often, you'll experience system instability and huge delays.
This is the SSD, it's where you store data that lasts a long time, unlike RAM, it will retain data for way longer.
This is the PSU, it's the component that provides the pc with electricity, without it, your pc will still be useless.
